In A Flash

~ Capturing the beat of the moment ~

Description

In a flash is a photography event where the participants will have to choose a particular theme and click a picture. Enter this free online photography competition to mark your talent. Photograpy is not just a word its an art, its an emotion for creators of all kinds. So take up this opportunity to portray your talent in a better way possible.

Themes

  • ● Nature

  • ● Life in lockdown

  • ● Pets or Animals

  • ● Under the roof

    • Rules & Regulations

    • ● Basic editing, including color enhancement, the use of filters and cropping of the photo is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the photo.

    • ● Photo submitted must be photographer’s own work. In order to verify that, we require the participants to attach a video (3-5 seconds) of the object/scene that is captured.

    • ● Participants are requested to send the photo and video on or before 24/06/2021. Responses sent after 24th June will not be validated.

    • ● It is an individual event.

    • ● Top 3 winners will be chosen by the judge and they will be awarded elite certificates

    • ● E-Mail Your responses to mail id given below.
