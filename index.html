Event Set - 1

Quizpicable Me

This event paves an easy way to keep track of progress from both logical and reasoning perspectives. This helps students to focus on and gain insight into the knowledge gaps. There are different kinds of questions used. Some examples include fill-in-the blank, multiple choice, and true or false questions.

Blow Hot Blow Cold

Blow hot blow cold, (also known as Block & Tackle) is a break from the conventional form of debating. Here, you need to speak both for and against the topic. You start off with “tackle” i.e., speaking ‘for’ the topic for about 1 minute and then you “block”, i.e., you need to reverse your stance and speak ‘against’ it for another 1 minute.

Rays of Rhythm

Rays of rhythm is a dance event where you can dance (any dance form) to any appropriate song (any language) of your choice. This is a great opportunity for students who like to excel in any art forms or dance forms. Take this chance to capture and showcase your talent. Make a video of the same and send it to us!

Event Set - 2

In A Flash

In a flash is a photography event where the participants will have to choose a particular theme and click a picture. Enter this free online photography competition to mark your talent. Photograpy is not just a word its an art, its an emotion for creators of all kinds. So take up this opportunity to portray your talent in a better way possible.

Kozy Kitchen

Kozy Kitchen is all about food plating. Food presentation is the best way for a chef to bring their personality into a dish. It is the art of decorating food to enhance its aesthetic appeal. With a little extra time spent plating, you can create a masterpiece on the plate. Lets discover new culinary talents from the participating schools.

Quarant Roll

Quarant Roll is a poster making event. This event is an opportunity for you to kick the boredom away and bring out the artist in you. The best part is that, you get to sketch a poster of your favourite movie or web series. Posters provide visual, colourful, simple ways to communicate your ideas to the public.
