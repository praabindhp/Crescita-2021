Quizpicable Me

~ Unlocking knowledge at the speed of thoughts ~

Description

This event paves an easy way to keep track of progress from both logical and reasoning perspectives. This helps students to focus on and gain insight into the knowledge gaps. There are different kinds of questions used. Some examples include fill-in-the blank, multiple choice, and true or false questions.

Round - 1

● This round will have 20 questions.

● The questions will be covering basics of mathematics, english, logical & reasoning and general knowledge.

● Questions will either be MCQ or Descriptive.

Round - 2

● Participants who score more than 50% in the first round will be qualified for the second round.

● This round will also have 20 questions, from the above mentioned categories.

● The rate of difficulty will be a little higher than the first round.
