Kozy Kitchen

~ Beyond the boundaries of taste ~

Description

Kozy Kitchen is all about food plating. Food presentation is the best way for a chef to bring their personality into a dish. It is the art of decorating food to enhance its aesthetic appeal. With a little extra time spent on plating, you can create a masterpiece on the plate. Lets discover new culinary talents from the participating schools.

Points To Remember

  • ● Cooking part can be done with the guidance of elders.

  • ● Plating can be done on any available cutlery.

  • ● Decorating the cooked food is the vital part that will be taken into consideration for judging.

  • ● There are no restrictions with respect to the cuisine or course.

    • Judging Elements

  • ● Colour

  • ● Presentation

  • ● Creativity

  • ● Decoration

  • ● Garnish

    • Rules & Regulations

    • ● This is an individual event.

    • ● The picture of the plated food should be clicked with proper lighting.

    • ● Participants are expected to do the plating by themselves.

    • ● In order to check the genuineness, participants are requested make a very short video of the plating process(5 seconds)

    • ● Kindly submit the picture and video proof before 24th June 2021 to the mail id given below.

    • ● Elite certificates will be provided for the top 3 winners.
